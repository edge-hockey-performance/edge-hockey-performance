The Gap in Competitive Hockey

Competitive hockey has
a blade management problem.

Pro shops and automated machines were built for walk-in convenience — not team workflows, not performance consistency. That gap shows up on the ice, every single skate.

No blade management system for competitive teams

There is no organized solution for managing blade sharpening across a full roster. Programs navigate the same walk-in counter as recreational skaters — no structure, no priority, no system.

No team-level pickup or delivery workflow

Families manage individual drop-off around practices and games. Steel gets forgotten, returned late, or picked up mid-rush. Coaches have no visibility into whether their roster is ready to skate.

Zero per-player blade history or tracking

Hollows, blade condition, wear history — none of it is recorded by player, by team, or across a season. Programs have no data on their own steel. Nothing carries over between visits.

Inconsistency compounds across a season

Different technicians, different machines, different pressure each week. For a team training three or four times a week, that inconsistency builds — and it shows up in how players perform on the ice.

Blade Education

Most players are skating on
inconsistent blades.

Sharpening is one of the most important parts of performance, yet it’s managed inconsistently at the youth level. Players bounce between pro shops. Hollow preferences change. Profiles wear down. No one is tracking anything.

Result: unpredictable edges, inconsistent feel, and performance that changes skate to skate. Understanding your steel is the first step to owning it.

How It Works

Three steps.
Zero friction.

Designed around your team's ice schedule — not a shop's hours or a family's availability.

1
Drop Off Blades

Players leave their steel runners in the team’s EDGE system before scheduled sessions — aligned to your practice and game calendar. No full skates. No counter wait. No family coordination.

2
We Sharpen + Track

Each sharpening is completed to spec and logged to the player’s profile. Edge symmetry is verified. Condition assessed. Every detail recorded automatically.

3
Skate With Confidence

Every player steps on the ice with consistent, verified edges — sharpened to their exact profile, performance-ready every session. No guesswork. No follow-up required from staff or families.
